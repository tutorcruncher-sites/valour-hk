TUTOR CODE OF CONDUCT

9. The Tutor will comply with the Tutor Code of Conduct set out in paragraphs a to q below. If the Tutor is unable to, or has not complied, they shall notify Valour immediately. The Tutor shall notify Valour of any complaints made by a Client regarding the tutoring.

a. The Tutor shall provide Valour with information on his/her educational background, work experience, and references and a full vulnerable criminal background check (enhanced), dated within the past 3 years, prior to commencing work for the Client and Students. Valour may provide prospective clients with information on the Tutor, such as resume, work experience, and educational background and any other information that the Client may reasonably request from Valour.

b. The Tutor warrants that all information given to the Client (via Valour or otherwise) whether prior to or during the provision of tuition is true and accurate in all respects.

c. The Tutor will maintain good relations with the Client, Student and the employees of Valour, ensuring the Tutor is highly approachable. The Tutor will encourage open and honest communication between all concerned parties.

d. The Tutor will only use appropriate language (full English), not slang. If another language is utilised the Tutor shall do so within the cultural pleasantries of that language. The Tutor will refer to Client formally with “Ms”/“Mrs”/“Mr” Jones unless otherwise requested.

e. The Tutor will be consistently punctual and thorough in their assignments to ensure a positive relationship with the Client and Student.

f. The Tutor will always use encouraging terminology and be pleasant to work with. The Tutor will be accommodating and flexible, not obstructive or uncooperative.

g. The Tutor will maintain a positive attitude, never showing signs of frustration or anger. Any Student misbehaving shall be dealt with using necessary behaviour management measures including but not limited to setting expectations, providing engaging work, communicating with parents.

h. The Tutor will adopt a “no touching policy” with the Client and Student where reasonable. If physical contact is necessary, then the Tutor will do so professionally and wholly appropriately. Any physical contact must be minimal and fulfil the purpose of learning.

i. The Tutor will be mindful of their appearance, considering the Client’s personal or religious views towards appropriate dress-code. The Tutor will ensure they are well presented so that no undergarments are visible, and their clothes are not crumpled.

j. The Tutor will always offer to remove their shoes upon entering a client’s home unless otherwise informed by the Client.

k. The Tutor understand that their proximity to Clients and Students requires impeccable personal hygiene. This includes but is not limited to regular showering, deodorant and teeth brushing.

l. The Tutor will plan and implement the appropriate course of study for the Student, including detailing the methods, and/or tutorial plans to be used. The Tutor will research all exam boards and syllabus in order to ensure they tailor their teaching towards the Student’s relevant concerns and topics to be addressed. The Tutor will always be fully prepared for tutorials by bringing necessary equipment, homework and resources.

m. If the Tutor sets homework, they will mark the previous tutorial’s homework in between tutorials. The Tutor will then utilise lesson time giving appropriate feedback. The Tutor will not engage in any form of plagiarism, such as completing pupils' homework assignments.

n. The Tutor will not engage in unnecessary conversations unrelated to learning during times of tuition. If such conversations begin, the Tutor will refocus the Student on work and resume the conversation at a more suitable time.

o. The Tutor will inform the Client and Valour of any availability issues 48 hours before the scheduled commitment. If there is an emergency, the Tutor will inform their clients and Valour immediately.

p. At the end of each tutorial the Tutor will write a detailed report outlining the timings and content of the tutorial as well as the performance of the Student. The Tutor will set any homework and the expected deadline for this as appropriate. This report will be uploaded to the Tutor Cruncher on the same day as the tutorial. If the Tutor fails to do so Valour has the right to withhold payment until this has been done.

q. The Tutor will log their hours on their Tutor Cruncher on the same day as the tutorial. The Tutor understand failure to do so will result in a delay in their payment.
