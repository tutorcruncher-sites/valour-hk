Privacy Policy

WHO ARE ‘WE’

When using ‘we’ or ‘us’ in the following policy we are referring to Valour HK LTD and any businesses we add in the future.



INFORMATION TYPE

"Personal information" is any information that we can use to identify an individual. This includes your name, phone number and address. It does not include encoded personal information, or publicly available information not combined with non-public information.



THE INFORMATION WE COLLECT

For utilisation of our services we require the following information:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Postal address
  • Username and password
  • Telephone
  • Date of birth
  • Demographic info
  • Payment information along with Bank account details
  • Communication preferences
  • Primary language


For a tutor account, we additionally require:

  • Subjects of expertise
  • Gender, personal statement, availability, qualifications and experience
  • Photo identification
  • DBS certificate
  • Contact Details of two references
  • Profile photo


USE OF INFORMATION

Your information is used to provide products, offers, services, or other content tailored to your interests. This may entail use in the following ways:

  • Answer questions and respond to requests
  • Develop new products or services
  • Manage your account
  • Process payments
  • Send you personalised emails with information we think of interest to you.


THIRD PARTY INFORMATION SHARING

Trust is pivotal to our business and as such no information is passed onto any third parties.


WEBSITE

The owners and moderators of this website ensure user privacy by taking the necessary steps to protect your privacy. This website additionally operates within all UK laws and requirements. 



  1. Cookies: Cookies are used to better your experience while visiting the website. Cookies are small text files placed on your device, used to track usage patterns and record preferences. Valour does not use cookies for personal information storage, but does to track usage of all visitors. If you wish to deny cookies on this website, please take necessary steps within your web browsers security settings to block all cookies.
  2. Analytics: Google Analytics is integrated within our site to enable us to develop the site and content around your interests. If you wish to opt-out of analytics, please consult your web browser guidance on the process. No personal information is stored, saved or collected.
  3. External Links: Whilst we ensure all external links are safe, please be cautious of utilising any external web links mentioned throughout this website. We cannot guarantee or verify the safety or content of any external links provided. You therefore use such links at your own risk. This website and its owners cannot be held liable for any damages or implications caused by visiting any external links mentioned.


RIGHT TO ACCESS AND DELETION

You may access the personal information that we hold about you (as defined in the EU General Protection Data Regulation 2018) once we receive a written request. Similarly, you may request limitation to the processing of your data, or deletion of any data by written request.

Before providing this information, we require proof of identity and sufficient information of your interactions with us.

If any of your personal information we hold is inaccurate or out of date, you may ask us to correct it.


COMPLAINTS

If you believe we have violated applicable law in the collection, storage or processing of your data, please contact us as described below. If we failed to remedy such violation satisfactorily, you may lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.


TIMEFRAME OF INFORMATION STORAGE

We retain your personal information. This ensures maximum quality of engagement for you. We will not store your information for longer than necessary.
